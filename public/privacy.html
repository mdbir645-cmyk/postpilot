What we collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we receive the profile information you approve (open ID, display name, avatar) and an access token that lets PostPilot publish content on your behalf. We store the video files you upload, their captions, and the privacy and interaction settings you choose for each post.

How we use it

We use this information only to operate the scheduling and publishing features you request: showing your queued posts, calling TikTok's Content Posting API at the time you choose, and reporting the result back to you. We do not sell this data or share it with advertisers.

How long we keep it

Access and refresh tokens are kept until you log out or revoke access from your TikTok account settings. Uploaded videos are kept so you can review what was posted; you can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Your controls

You can disconnect PostPilot from your TikTok account at any time from TikTok's app settings, which revokes our access token immediately. Contact [your support email] to request a copy or deletion of your data.
