What PostPilot does

PostPilot lets you upload video content, schedule a publish time, and publish it to your own TikTok account using TikTok's official Content Posting API. You control the caption, audience setting, and interaction options for every post before it goes out.

Account access

Connecting your TikTok account grants PostPilot permission to publish on your behalf, limited to the scopes shown on TikTok's consent screen. You can revoke this at any time from TikTok's app settings.
